About Us

Inspiration Info Tech was established in the year 2015 as a sole propriety establishment and converted to a Limited Liability Company under the nameInspiration Info Tech Electronic Industries Company.

A multi-brand seller & distributor of retail, industrial, engineering & office furniture solutionsInspiration Info Tech Electronic Industries Co. is one of the few Saudi companies to obtain ISO - 9001:2015 certification.

Inspiration Info Tech supplies & supports industry leading products ranging from Retail POS Solutions to Access Control Products, Office Furniture Solutions, Time Attendance Systems, CCTV & Biometric Devices, Metal detectors & Antishop Lifting Systems, Queuing Systems, Kiosks, Money counting machines, fake money detectors & many more products & services.

Driven by Innovation & Customer care,Inspiration Info Tech provides qualified professional services to a wide customer base ranging from small shops to large enterprises such as Aramco, National Water Company, SAMBA, Zain, Abdul Latif Jameel and most Ministries and Embassies.

We are even proud to have achieved the necessary qualifications to become a Microsoft Certified Partner and also are associated with Datalogic as a Gold Partner. Additionally, we have formed partnerships with industry leaders such as Kisan, Toshiba, Epson, Data logic, TSC, DIGI, Evolis and many such more.

Inspiration Info Tech Electronics can serve your esteemed company in the whole of Saudi Arabia with its presence across corporate office in Riyadh, and other offices in Jeddah & Khobar.
